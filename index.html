Albert Einstein

a person with unbelievable imaginary power

  • March 14, 1879 - Albert Einstein is born in Ulm, Germany, the first child of Hermann and Pauline Einstein.
  • June 21, 1880The Einstein family moves to Munich, Germany.
  • March 31, 1885Einstein enrolls in the second grade of a Catholic elementary school called the Petersschule. He receives Jewish religious instruction at home and also begins taking violin lessons.
  • October 1, 1888路Einstein is accepted at the Luitpold Gymnasium
  • June 1894The Einstein family moves to Milan, Italy, but Einstein remains in Munich to complete his education at the Gymnasium.
  • October 26, 1895After failing the entry exam for the Zurich Polytechnic, Einstein is accepted to the trade department of the cantonal school in Aarau, Switzerland. He lives with the family of Jost Winteler, a teacher in Aarau.
  • January 6, 1903Einstein and Mileva are married in Bern.
  • May 14, 1904Hans Albert, Einstein and Mileva's first son, is born in Bern.
  • March 17, 1905Einstein completes his paper on quantum theory.
  • September 28, 1905路Einstein's paper on the special theory of relativity is published in the Annalen der Physik.
  • January 15, 1906Einstein receives his doctorate from the University of Zurich.
  • May 7, 1909Einstein is appointed extraordinary professor of theoretical physics at Zurich University.
  • February 14, 1919Einstein and Mileva obtain a divorce.
  • June 2, 1919Einstein marries his cousin Elsa in Berlin.
  • April 2-May 30 1921Einstein visits the U.S. for the first time on a fund- raising tour for the establishment of the Hebrew University in Jerusalem.
  • November 9, 1922Einstein wins the Nobel Prize for his work on quantum theory.
  • October 17, 1933路Einstein moves to the United States with his wife and his secretary Helen Dukas.
  • August 2, 1939instein signs a letter to President Franklin D. Roosevelt urging the acceleration of atomic bomb development.
  • October 1, 1940Einstein becomes an American citizen.
  • November 1952After Chaim Weizmann's death, Einstein is offered the presidency of Israel. He declines.
  • April 18, 1955路Einstein dies in New Jersey. His body is cremated and his papers turned over to the Hebrew University in Jerusalem.
